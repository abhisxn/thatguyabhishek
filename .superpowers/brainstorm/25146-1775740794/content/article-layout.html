A — Centered long-form

Minimal, editorial layout. Large centred title block, then prose in a narrow reading column (~65ch). No sidebar. Matches the writing itself — stripped back, no distractions. Closest to Medium or Substack feel but dark and on-brand.

B — Hero banner + sidebar

Gradient hero banner (matches project page pattern), then two-column layout: sticky sidebar with topic tags + "more articles" on the left, prose on the right. More structured, easier to navigate between articles. Closer to existing work/ page feel.

C — Editorial with hook

Left-aligned header with emoji + title inline. Then the Hook field (from Notion) displayed as a styled pull-quote before the body — gives readers a taste before they commit. Single-column prose below. Premium, journalistic feel. My recommendation.
