Desktop sidebar — how should it behave?

The article body is centered at 720px max-width. The sidebar sits to the left of it. Pick the interaction model that feels right.

A

Always visible, fixed

Sidebar is present from the moment the article loads. Fixed position, scrolls with you. Active section highlights as you read. Clean and persistent — like linear.app docs or Notion's page outline.

B

Appears after scrolling past the header

Hidden initially — fades in once you scroll past the article title. Keeps the hero clean, then guides you through the body. Disappears when you reach the footer.

C

Minimal dots, expands on hover

Shows only small progress dots by default. Hovering reveals the full sidebar with section labels. Very subtle — barely there unless you need it.

Sidebar anatomy (all options)

Active section — brand color, full opacity
Inactive section — muted, lighter weight
Track line — subtle vertical rail connecting items
